About Us

PocketFlash is a simple platform that focuses on new learning ways of contemporary society.
The user can get instant access to their thousands of flashcards within no time lapse once they created their accounts.

A classy tool to store your short notes in the form of flashcards.


  • There comes a point in your learning efforts where you’ve simply got to face the facts – literally – by acknowledging that memorising details are an essential part of many types of knowledge acquisition. PocketFlash is the perfect learning tool to help you retain essential information, no matter what your subject, level, or field of study.

  • PocketFlash is perfect for learning important info quickly! Memorize verbs, grammar rules, historical dates, quotes, formulas and lots more.

  • The combination of text and visual elements enhances your ability to recall information. You can easily add images to your flashcards – this has obvious benefits for subjects such as art, but images can also help to reinforce your learning of text or numerical data – such as key dates in history.
